About me

Hi, I am András Hann.
Me at the end of the 1160-kilometer-long Blue Trail in Hollóháza on February 14, 2017. Born and raised in Budapest, I’m currently working as a Data Scientist at Booking.com in Amsterdam.

This website is not a frequently updated blog; I use it to showcase personal projects that I work on in my free time. You can get in touch with me by sending an email to [andras at this domain] or via LinkedIn.

You might notice the simple theme – I chose it so that it does not distract from the content of the website. It is inspired by Edward Tufte’s work, who laid down numerous suggetions for best practices of presenting information. His most famous book is The Visual Display of Quantitative Information.
